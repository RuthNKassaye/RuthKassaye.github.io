Preparing for influenza season in the USA

The United States has an influenza season where more people than usual suffer from flu. Some people, particularly those in vulnerable populations, develop serious complications and end up in hospital. Therefore, hospitals and clinics need additional staff to adequately treat these extra patients. In this project, I analyzed trends in influenza and determined where and when to send medical staff based on each State's vulnerability to flu using Excel and created dashboard with Tableau.
